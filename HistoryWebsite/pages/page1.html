First Meeting of the High Level Working Group, 2010

Event Details

The First Meeting of the High Level Working Group took place in Belize City, Belize on March 9, 2010. This meeting marked a significant step in addressing the territorial dispute between Belize and Guatemala. The High Level Working Group was established to explore confidence-building measures (CBMs) and facilitate dialogue between the two nations. This initial meeting laid the groundwork for further discussions and initiatives aimed at peacefully resolving the longstanding border conflict.
